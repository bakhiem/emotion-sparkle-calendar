Design preferences for MoodFlow emotion tracker app

- Style: Cute, soft pastel, nature-inspired, playful & cozy, 2D flat
- Fonts: Quicksand (body) + Comfortaa (display/headings)
- Palette: Nature greens, sky blues, warm earth tones, soft pastels
- Cards: `.nature-card` class — rounded-2xl, subtle border, soft shadow
- Icons: Custom kawaii leaf characters with faces (happy→crying expressions)
- Tone: Gentle, nature-themed copy ("How are you feeling? 🌿")
- NO 3D effects, no perspective transforms
- Decorative: Soft pastel blur blobs in background
